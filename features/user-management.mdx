title: User management
description: How to view and manage users, roles, and accounts — plus seller metrics, client segmentation, vendor moderation, and system configuration.

User management spans several admin routes, from the main user table to per-role analytics and vendor controls. All of these pages require the **admin** role.

## Viewing and filtering users (`/admin/usuarios`)

The user management page shows a paginated table of every registered account on the platform.

### Table columns

| Column | What is shown |
|---|---|
| **Usuario** | Full name and email address |
| **Rol** | Colour-coded badge: purple = Admin, blue = Seller, green = Client |
| **Estado** | Green **Activo** or red **Inactivo** badge |
| **Registro** | Account creation date (Colombia locale) |
| **Último Acceso** | Most recent login date |
| **Acciones** | Edit-role and toggle-active controls |

### Filtering

Two controls appear above the table:

- **Search box** — filters by name or email in real time.
- **Role dropdown** — narrows results to Administrators, Sellers, or Clients; defaults to "All roles".

The result count updates live: `Total: N usuarios`.

### Role assignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the role editor">
    Click the edit (pencil) icon in the **Acciones** column for the user you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the new role">
    A dropdown replaces the role badge inline. Choose **Administrador**, **Vendedor**, or **Cliente**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the change">
    Click **Guardar**. A success notification confirms the update. Click **Cancelar** to discard the change without saving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Changing a user's role to **admin** grants full access to the admin panel immediately. Make sure you intend to elevate the account before saving.
</Warning>

### Activating and deactivating accounts

Click the toggle icon in the **Acciones** column to flip a user's active state. Active accounts show a green toggle; inactive accounts show red. The platform shows a success notification confirming the change (`Usuario [name] activado/desactivado`).

### Role summary cards

Below the table three cards show the total count of Administrators, Sellers, and Clients currently in the system.

## Seller metrics (`/admin/vendedores`)

This page gives you a comparative view of every seller's performance.

### Bar chart comparison

A `recharts` `BarChart` renders at the top, comparing total sales (COP) per seller by first name.

### Seller performance table

| Column | What is shown |
|---|---|
| **Vendedor** | Avatar, full name, and email |
| **Ventas Totales** | Cumulative revenue attributed to this seller |
| **Pedidos** | Total orders with completed sub-count |
| **Ticket Promedio** | Average order value |
| **Reportes** | Quality/bug report count, coloured green (0), yellow (1–2), or red (3+) |
| **Estado** | Active or Inactive badge |

Click any row to expand a detail panel showing four cards: Ingresos Generados, Pedidos Completados (+ pending), Ticket Promedio, and Reportes Recibidos with percentage rate. Click the same row again to collapse it.

### Summary row

Three cards below the table surface the top seller, average sales per seller, and total quality reports across all sellers.

## Client metrics (`/admin/clientes`)

This page segments clients by lifetime spend and shows your top 10 highest-value customers.

### Segmentation cards

| Segment | Threshold |
|---|---|
| **VIP** | Total spent > $1,000,000 COP |
| **Regular** | Total spent $300,000 – $1,000,000 COP |
| **Nuevo** | Total spent < $300,000 COP |

Each card shows the count and aggregate value for that segment.

### Top 10 clients table

Sorted by total spend, each row shows:

- Position (gold/silver/bronze badges for top 3)
- Name and email
- Total spent
- Number of orders
- Average order value
- Purchase frequency (e.g., "Every 14 days" or "First purchase")
- Date of last purchase

### Summary stats

Four cards below the table show average client value, average purchases per client, best client name + amount, and platform-wide average ticket.

## Vendor moderation — approval queue (`/admin/moderacion/aprobacion`)

When a seller submits a new product it enters the approval queue with a **Pendiente** status. You must approve or reject it before it appears in the public catalog.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter by seller (optional)">
    Use the seller dropdown at the top right to focus on one vendor's submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the product">
    Click **Ver** on a product card to open a full-screen preview with the product image, name, price, description, and seller info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or reject">
    Click **Aprobar** to publish the product to the catalog immediately. Click **Rechazar** to open the rejection modal, enter a reason, then confirm. The seller sees your rejection reason so they can fix the listing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Approving a product triggers a catalog refresh (`refreshProducts`) so the change is visible in the storefront immediately.
</Note>

When the queue is empty, the page shows a confirmation message: "¡Todo al día! No hay productos pendientes de aprobación".

## Vendor performance (`/admin/moderacion/vendedores`)

This page combines a top-5 sales comparison chart with a management table that gives you direct control over seller commissions and account status.

### Summary cards

Four cards show: total sellers, active sellers, suspended sellers, and the platform-wide average commission rate.

### Dual-axis bar chart

A `recharts` `BarChart` with two Y-axes plots the top 5 sellers simultaneously on sales volume (thousands COP, purple bars) and order count (amber bars).

### Commission rate editing

Click the commission percentage shown in the **Comisión %** column to activate an inline number input. Press Enter or click away to save. The rate accepts decimal values.

### Suspending a seller

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the toggle control">
    In the **Control** column, click the red toggle icon next to an active seller.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a suspension reason">
    A modal prompts you to explain the reason (e.g., "Multiple quality reports"). The reason is stored and displayed on the seller's row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirmar Suspensión**. The seller's row becomes semi-transparent and their status badge changes to **Suspendido**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reactivate">
    Click the green toggle icon on a suspended seller to immediately reactivate their account (no reason prompt required).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Suspending a seller prevents them from accessing their dashboard. Ensure you have reviewed their quality report rate and sales history before taking action.
</Warning>

## System configuration (`/admin/configuracion`)

This page lets you control global platform settings. All changes take effect after you click **Guardar Configuración**.

### Configuration sections

<Accordion title="General information">
  Set the **Nombre del Sitio** (site name) that appears in the browser tab and platform headers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Theme colours">
  Pick **Color Primario**, **Color Secundario**, and **Color de Acento** using colour pickers or hex input fields. Changes affect button colours, link colours, and accent elements throughout the storefront.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sales settings">

  | Field | Purpose |
  |---|---|
  | **Tasa de Impuesto (%)** | Tax rate applied at checkout (e.g., 19 for Colombia IVA) |
  | **Costo de Envío ($)** | Flat shipping fee in COP |
  | **Umbral Envío Gratis ($)** | Order value above which shipping is waived |
  | **Umbral Stock Bajo (metros)** | Global low-stock threshold in metres (also used in inventory alerts) |

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Global banner">
  Toggle a sitewide announcement banner on or off, set its message text, and choose the banner type:

  - **Información** — blue banner
  - **Éxito** — green banner
  - **Advertencia** — yellow banner

  A live preview renders below the form when the banner is enabled.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Display preferences">
  Check **Activar modo oscuro por defecto para nuevos usuarios** to default new accounts to dark mode.
</Accordion>
