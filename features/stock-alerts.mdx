---
title: Stock alerts
description: How sellers monitor low-stock and out-of-stock products in D&D Textil.
---

The stock alerts page is available to sellers at `/vendedor/stock`. It surfaces inventory problems across three severity levels and also aggregates open quality reports that require the seller's attention.

## Alert thresholds

Stock levels are evaluated against two thresholds:

| Level | Condition | Label |
|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | `stock < 10 m` | Stock Crítico |
| **Low** | `10 m ≤ stock < lowStockThreshold` | Stock Bajo |
| **Out of stock** | `stock === 0 m` | Agotados |

The `lowStockThreshold` value defaults to **20 meters** and is read from `systemConfig` in `MetricsContext`. An administrator can adjust this system-wide setting.

<Note>
  A product with `stock = 0` counts in **both** the critical and out-of-stock buckets in the summary cards, but it is only listed once — under the Agotados section.
</Note>

## Summary cards

Four cards at the top of the page give counts for the current seller's products:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stock Crítico" icon="trending-down">
    Products with fewer than 10 meters remaining. Red left border. Requires urgent action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stock Bajo" icon="archive">
    Products between 10 m and the `lowStockThreshold`. Orange left border. Plan for restocking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agotados" icon="package">
    Products with zero meters available. Grey left border.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reportes Abiertos" icon="alert-circle">
    Open quality or support reports filed by clients against this seller's products. Yellow left border.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Critical stock section

When any products have fewer than 10 meters, a red-highlighted section appears with the heading **Stock Crítico - Acción Urgente**.

Each item shows:
- Product thumbnail
- Product name
- Internal reference ID
- Price per meter
- Current stock in meters (displayed in red)
- **Reponer Urgente** action button

## Low stock section

When any products are between 10 m and the threshold, the **Stock Bajo - Planificar Reposición** section lists them in the same format, with the stock count displayed in orange and a **Reponer Stock** action button.

## Out-of-stock section

Products with `stock = 0` appear in the **Productos Agotados** section. Items are rendered at reduced opacity with a greyscale product image to indicate they are unavailable. The stock display reads **AGOTADO** in grey and a **Reponer** button is shown.

## Open quality reports

Below the inventory sections, the page lists any quality or support reports (bug reports) filed against this seller's products that are still in `open` status.

Each report card shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Title | Short description of the issue |
| Description | Full client-reported problem text |
| Priority badge | **Alta** (red), **Media** (yellow), or **Baja** (blue) |
| Product | Name of the affected product |
| Client | Name of the client who filed the report |
| Date | Date the report was submitted |

<Warning>
  High-priority reports (tagged **Alta**) indicate issues like wrong fabric, staining, or incorrect meters. Respond to these first to avoid escalation.
</Warning>

## All clear state

If no critical, low, or out-of-stock items exist and there are no open reports, the page displays a green confirmation panel: **¡Todo en Orden!** — indicating no alerts require attention at this time.
